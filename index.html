About

I am currently pursuing game development both independently and as a volunteer within a small studio, Break Knight Games, focusing on Unreal Engine and C++ to build gameplay systems, tools, and prototypes. Alongside my game development work, I bring one year of experience as a Software Engineer Consultant, where I contributed to client projects that required full‑stack development, problem‑solving, and adaptability in fast‑paced environments.

My educational background includes earning a Bachelor’s degree in Game Development from Full Sail University, where I graduated as the Salutatorian and built a strong foundation in Unreal Engine, C++, programming, and game design principles. I also completed an intensive full‑stack web development bootcamp, which strengthened my understanding of modern development workflows and technologies.

Before transitioning into tech, I served for five years in U.S. Army Military Intelligence. That experience shaped my discipline, attention to detail, and ability to perform under pressure — qualities that continue to influence my work as a developer.
