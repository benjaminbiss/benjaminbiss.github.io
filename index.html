About

I hold a Bachelor's degree in Game Development from Full Sail University, where I built a strong foundation in programming, design, and interactive media. I’ve spent the past three years exploring game development as an indie developer, creating interactive experiences and refining my technical and creative skills.

Professionally, I’ve worked as a Software Engineer Consultant, contributing to client projects that required full‑stack development, problem‑solving, and an agile mindset. I also completed an intensive full‑stack web development bootcamp, deepening my experience with modern web technologies and development workflows.

Before transitioning into tech, I served for five years in U.S. Army Military Intelligence — an experience that strengthened my discipline, attention to detail, and ability to perform under pressure.
